Summary

India's quantum computing ecosystem includes two prominent companies: QNu Labs¹, a Bengaluru-based startup focused on quantum-safe cryptography, and BosonQ Psi², offering quantum simulation platforms. IISc's Centre for Quantum Technology³ also has active industry collaboration programs in superconducting qubit research. All three are operating at TRL 4–6 as of March 2026.
