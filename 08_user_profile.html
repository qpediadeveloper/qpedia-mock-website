About

Dr. Priya Sharma is an Assistant Professor at the Centre for Quantum Technology, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. Her research focuses on quantum error correction, with a particular emphasis on surface code implementations for superconducting qubit platforms.

She leads the Fault-Tolerant Quantum Computing group at IISc, working on logical qubit encoding, decoding algorithm design, and hardware-aware QEC protocols. Her group collaborates with IBM Quantum and the National Quantum Mission on applied error correction research.

Prior to joining IISc, Dr. Sharma completed her postdoctoral fellowship at Caltech's Institute for Quantum Information and Matter, where she worked on topological quantum codes under Prof. John Preskill.
